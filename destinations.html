Goa, India

Goa is known for its vibrant beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese colonial influence. Best time: Nov–Feb.

Paris, France

Paris, the City of Lights, offers romance, art, cafes, museums, and the iconic Eiffel Tower. Best time: Apr–Jun.

Kyoto, Japan

Historic temples, cherry blossoms, and serene gardens make Kyoto a peaceful destination. Best time: Mar–May, Oct–Nov.

Dubai, UAE

From skyscrapers to deserts, Dubai is the hub of luxury and innovation. Best time: Nov–Mar.

Maldives

A tropical paradise with overwater villas, coral reefs, and crystal-clear waters. Best time: Dec–Apr.

Switzerland

Snowy Alps, scenic train rides, chocolates, and lakes – a dreamy destination for nature lovers.
